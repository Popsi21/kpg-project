Why Koh Phangan?

Koh Phangan, the Crystal Island

Rumours have it that Koh Phangan is a ‘Crystal Island’, formed from a giant Quartz crystal. We have spoken about this in the office many times. Koh Phangan has a particular energy. When you are up, you are super up and when you are down, then you are really down. There seems to be no middle ground, though some may have found it, and hopefully while you read this, you are in the ‘super up there’ category.
